Broccoli Mom

Kayla Blackburn is the vocalist and guitarist of Broccoli Mom. Drawing inspiration from songwriters like Patty Griffin and Tracy Chapman, her songwriting pulls you in and remind you of the profound, yet painful human experience. She is an audio engineer graduating from Berklee College of Music in December 2021. Her musical background prior to her audio education consists of songwriting and classical voice training. Her classical background has made her passionate about choral music, exploring the intersection of choral arrangements and technology.
